Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 24, 2023

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our services. We are committed to maintaining the trust and confidence of our visitors.

§ 01

Data We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including when you create an account, make a purchase, or communicate with us. This may include:

  • Identity Data: Name, username, or similar identifier.
  • Contact Data: Billing address, email address, and telephone numbers.
  • Technical Data: IP address, login data, browser type and version, and time zone setting.
§ 02

How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services. Specifically, we use your data for:

Service Delivery

To process your transactions and manage your account access efficiently.

Communication

To send you technical notices, updates, security alerts, and support messages.

Personalisation

To tailor our content and provide features that match your interests.

Security

To monitor and prevent fraudulent transactions and other illegal activities.

§ 03

Data Security

We have put in place appropriate security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost, used or accessed in an unauthorized way, altered or disclosed. We limit access to your personal data to those employees, agents, contractors and other third parties who have a business need to know.
§ 04

Cookies

You can set your browser to refuse all or some browser cookies, or to alert you when websites set or access cookies. If you disable or refuse cookies, please note that some parts of this website may become inaccessible or not function properly.

§ 05

Terms & Conditions

By placing an order with CustomiseMe UK, you agree to the following terms. Please read them carefully before purchasing.

  • Emergency Orders Last-minute emergency orders may be accepted at our discretion. Such orders will incur an additional charge of 50% of the total order value, applied at checkout. Emergency orders are collection only and are not eligible for delivery. The collection address will be provided via email once the order has been processed.
  • Use of Product Images By placing an order with us, you grant permission for us to use photographs or images of your completed product for promotional and marketing purposes. If you wish to opt out, please include the note "please do not use for promotion" in the additional information box at checkout.
  • Production Timeline Production will commence only after full payment has been received and confirmed.
  • Design Services If you require us to create a custom design or artwork, a consultation must be booked at least 3 days in advance. Once the design has been finalised, a separate order must be placed for the product.
  • Order Accuracy Customers are responsible for ensuring that all order details, including sizes, colours, spelling, and design specifications, are accurate at the time of purchase. We are not liable for errors submitted by the customer.
  • Returns & Refunds Due to the customised nature of our products, all sales are final. We do not accept returns or offer refunds unless the item is faulty or incorrect.
  • Turnaround Time Estimated production and processing times will be provided at the time of order. While we aim to meet all deadlines, delays may occur during busy periods.
  • Collection & Delivery Customers will be notified once their order is ready for collection or dispatch. We are not responsible for delays caused by third-party delivery services.
  • Cancellations & Amendments Orders cannot be cancelled or modified once production has begun. Any changes requested prior to production may be subject to additional charges.
